GTSRB Leaderboard

The GTSRB leaderboard compiles results from different backdoor defense methods against different backdoor attack methods. For different poisoning ratio settings, please use the tabs below to navigate to the corresponding leaderboard, which measures the robustness of a model.

Metric:

  • CA (Clean Accuracy): The ratio of clean samples that are identified as their ground-truth classes,, which reflects the healthiness of a model.
  • ASR (Attack Success Rate): The ratio of poisoned samples that are identified as the target class, which manifests the immunity of a model.
  • RA (Robust Accuracy): The ratio of poisoned samples that are identified as their ground-truth classes, which measures the robustness of a model.
