Metric:

  • C-Acc (Clean Accuracy): The ratio of clean samples that are identified as their ground-truth classes,, which reflects the healthiness of a model.

  • R-Acc (Robust Accuracy): The ratio of poisoned samples that are identified as their ground-truth classes, which measures the robustness of a model.
